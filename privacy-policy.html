Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

TikTok Uploader is a personal script for uploading videos to TikTok. This Privacy Policy outlines how data is handled.

1. Data Collection

This script collects OAuth tokens to authenticate with the TikTok API and video files for uploading. No personal data from other users is collected.

2. Data Use

OAuth tokens are used solely to authenticate and upload videos to your TikTok account. Video files are sent to TikTok’s API.

3. Third-Party Sharing

Data (e.g., videos, tokens) is shared with TikTok’s API to perform uploads, per their terms.

4. User Rights

You can revoke access via your TikTok account settings.
